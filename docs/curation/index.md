# Curation

Api3 operates as a vault curator on [Morpho,](https://morpho.org/) the largest decentralized lending protocol on Ethereum.
As a curator, Api3 selects lending markets, manages risk parameters, and allocates capital to generate yield for vault depositors.

## Api3 x Morpho

Morpho is a permissionless lending protocol that allows anyone to create isolated lending markets with custom parameters.
[Morpho Vaults](https://docs.morpho.org/morpho-vaults/concepts/vault/) are smart contracts that aggregate deposits and allocate them across these markets, abstracting the complexity of individual market selection for depositors.

A **curator** is the entity responsible for configuring and managing a Morpho Vault — deciding which markets to enable, setting supply caps, and ensuring the vault operates within its risk parameters.
Api3 operates as a curator for a set of USDC vaults on Ethereum mainnet.

## Skin in the game

Unlike most curators who manage third-party capital exclusively, Api3 deploys a majority of its own treasury as the primary supply in its vaults.
This means Api3 has direct financial exposure to every market it enables and every risk decision it makes.

External depositors are welcome to supply alongside the Api3 treasury, benefiting from the same risk management and yield strategies, with the assurance that Api3's own capital is subject to identical conditions.

## Yield

Yield in Api3 vaults comes from interest paid by borrowers on Morpho lending markets.
When borrowers take loans against their collateral, they pay a variable interest rate that is distributed to vault depositors proportionally.

For Api3 curated markets, the entirety of the [OEV](/oev/) generated by Api3 data feeds belongs to Api3, providing an additional source of protocol revenue.
